About

I'm a senior at Williams College majoring in Computer Science. This portfolio showcases several design sprints completed throughout the HCI course I took this semester. Each project explores how thoughtful design can better serve diverse users. From usability testing to accessibility audits, these projects reflect my commitment to empathetic, iterative, and inclusive design.

Design Sprints

DS1: Healthcare Appointment Usability

Interviewed users about challenges with healthcare appointment systems. Identified pain points and developed personas and journey maps to understand the complexity of scheduling interfaces.
